Pet Photoshoot - Discounted

To celebrate the launch of my business I am offering a mini photoshoot of your pet, (horse, cat, dog, you name it, ill shoot it)

The shoot will last approximately 45 minutes. The best images from the shoot will be shown, edited and provided to you for free via digital download. You will also be able to order hard prints of your images for a small price via the image portfolio site I use.

Photoshoots with Animals are best achieved outdoors, so if you have booked with me and it happens to be an awfully wet day, then we will postpone to a later date when, hopefully the weather will be more appealing. In some instances, this may mean rearranging several times until we are lucky with the weather.

If your creature is an indoor dweller, then I am happy to accommodate, it just means the range of images I am able to provide may be more limited in visual variety. But we can discuss creative ideas to suit your requirements prior to the shoot so I can capture your friend at their best.

If you are keen to book then please get in touch and I will get you scheduled in for a time that suits you. All bookings are made on a first come first serve basis, and payments will be required prior to the photoshoot.
