Candice Hudson-Rolf

After years of enjoying amateur photography, in 2021 I sought a career change to become a full time professional photographer. After extensive private tuition at the Hampshire School of Photography I launched my business and haven’t looked back since.

I am an avid equestrian, naturalist and country girl at heart. In between raising my children and running a business, you’ll often find me wandering the countryside with my dog, whatever the weather.

While I enjoy many varieties of photography, my speciality is portrait/lifestyle. I love the intimacy that this kind of photography captures, and I particularly like photographing pets in this setting. I work with both natural and studio lighting, depending on client and image requirements.

Here at CHR photography I can make even the most camera shy clients feel at ease in front of the camera. My aim is to produce quality bespoke images, tailor made to your requirements, which we will discuss in detail before the shoot. I have full public liability insurance.
